The equation for vertical motion is given by:
\begin{equation}
    z(t) = d_1 \cos(\omega t) + d_2 \sin(\omega t) + r(t) - \frac{g}{\omega^2}
\end{equation}
where
\begin{align*}
    d_1 &= z_0 - r_0 + \frac{g}{\omega^2}, \\
    d_2 &= \frac{\dot{z}}{\omega} - \frac{(r_T - r_0)}{T \omega}, \\
    \omega &= \sqrt{\frac{k}{m}}.
\end{align*}
